Shamir Secret Sharing Scheme

How to use:
  • To split a secret: Enter your secret, choose the number of parts and the threshold, then click Split Secret. Each part will appear in its own box. Copy and distribute each part to a different person.
  • To reconstruct the secret: Collect the required number of parts (at least the threshold). Paste each part (one per line) into the Combine section and click Combine Parts to reveal the original secret.
  • Security tip: Never share all parts with one person. The secret can only be reconstructed if the threshold number of parts are combined.
