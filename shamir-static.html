Shamir Secret Sharing Scheme

What is this? This tool lets you split a secret (like a password or recovery phrase) into multiple parts. Only a chosen number of those parts (the threshold) are needed to reconstruct the secret.

How to use:
  • Enter your secret (e.g. mySuperSecret123).
  • Set Number of parts to 5 and Threshold to 3.
  • Click Split Secret. Give each part to a different person.
  • To recover the secret, collect any 3 of the 5 parts and paste them (one per line) into the Combine section. The secret will appear automatically.
Security tip: Never give all parts to one person. The secret can only be reconstructed if the threshold number of parts are combined.
